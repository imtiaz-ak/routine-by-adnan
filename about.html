About

Joining online classes can be a hassle when you have to type in meeting ids and passwords three times a day. So I thought it would be convenient to make a website where you could just click the subject name and be redirected to the respective subject teacher's zoom meeting. I apologize if the website seems buggy. This is literally the first webpage I have ever made. I used the routine design Mariah sent to the Whatsapp group as a reference for the routine layout, so big thanks to her. Also props to Maheer for helping me collect the meeting links.
